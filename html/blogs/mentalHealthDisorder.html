Various Types Of Mental Health Disorders

Mental Health Disorders

Any adverse effect on our psychological, behavioral, emotional, social, and cognitive wellbeing is termed as mental health disorder or mental illness. Several types of mental diseases vary based on the reasons behind these disorders..


  • Anxiety disorders
  • Phobias
  • Mood disorders
  • Schizophrenia disorders
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Depression
  • Eating disorders
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Personality disorders
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Psychosis
  • Schizoaffective disorder
  • Self-harm
  • Suicidal feelings
