How to maintain Positive Mental Health

  • Starting your day with energy booster (Coffee)
  • Go for a half an hour of Morning Walk
  • Get professional help if it is needed
  • Always stay positive
  • Make connections with positive people
  • Keep yourself physically active
  • Develop Managing Skills
  • Get sufficient sleep
  • Help others
  • Add gratitude behavior in your life
  • Plan a getaway for at least eight weeks
  • Build self-confidence by doing what you love
  • Be creative and add new experiments in your life
  • Show love to your family, friends, and relatives
  • Dance all around while doing your hosework
  • Exercise forgiveness
  • Yawning also improves your brain efficiency and alertness
