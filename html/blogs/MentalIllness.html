Mental Illness Symptoms

  • Moving away from usual activities and relatives, friends, or even familiar people.
  • The feeling of little or no energy.
  • Eating too much or too little.
  • Having a hopeless or helpless situation.
  • Using drugs, drinking, or smoking.
  • Hearing sudden voices.
  • Being incapable of completing daily assignments.
  • Feeling emotionless like nobody matters.
  • Feeling strangely forgetful, angry, worried, upset, scared, on edge, or confused.
  • Experiencing problem in relationships due to severe swings in mood.
  • Fighting and yelling at friends and family.
  • Faintness to accomplish daily tasks.
  • Continuously thinking of harming others or yourself.
