What is Mindfulness

A mental state achieved by focusing one's awareness on the present moment, while calmly acknowledging and accepting one's feelings, thoughts, and bodily sensations, used as a therapeutic technique.

Mental Health is a psychological, behavioral, emotional, social, and cognitive wellbeing. It depends on our relation to our thoughts as well as to others. How do we make daily life choices, how do we handle stress, and how we react to our surrounding, all these actions and reactions are related to our mental health.
